    Hexacube is a new commercial development at 160 Changi Road, consisting of 73 Office and Commercial Shop Space.

    Hexacube is a sparkling new landmark emerging just mere minutes from the bustling Paya Lebar Central. A new launch Freehold commercial building with a futuristic facade, it is just a few minutes walk away from Eunos MRT/Interchange and a close proximity to Paya Lebar Commercial Hub. Not only does it have an easy access to other parts of Singapore via public transport, it also boosts easy access to the AYE and PIE expressways. It only takes 15 minutes drive to the business district of Singapore or to the airport,ensuring that you are always connected to the business world or the global network.
    With a reputable developer none other than Lian Beng Group which is an A1 Grade contractor with the Building and Construction Authority (BCA) and a Singaporean home-grown building construction group with a solid track record in handling large-scale and complex projects, you can only expect the best quality and workmanship for this development.
    Hexacube @ Changi Road is a mixed commercial building which will be home to a wide range of enchanting retail outlets as well as a practical office hub flowing with energy and bursting with vibrancy. It will be the gateway to an exciting new world where business and pleasure meets – there will be three floors of retail and F&B outlets, and another three floors of office units. All floors add up to a total of 73 units for commercial use. We will change the way you spend your working hours by redefining the workplace and bring your commercial, recreational, retail and dining facilities all under one roof. You can expect a premium grade office hub with 3 levels of retail each other for an enviable work-life balance.
    Hexacube – A truly unique and exuberant development awaits you.
